Why did I enroll in CS50?

One of my biggest regrets in my university life is that I did not continue learning Python. Before I graduated from the university, I had a little hand-on experience with web designing using Adobe Dreamweaver in a school project, which ignited my interest in IT.

After I got my bachelor degree, I was thinking of pursuing my career in the field of IT. However, without a solid background, it is quite difficult for me to get a job. I ended up working as an IT business analyst in a bank, which is quite challenging for me as I do not have enough technical knowledge to communicate with developers. Therefore, to tackle with my weakness and try to explore more opportunities, here I am!
